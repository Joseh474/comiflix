The Story Behind Comiflix

Founded in early 2024 by a passionate collective of AI researchers, philosophers, theologians, and content creators, Comiflix emerged from late-night conversations about the limits of human discourse. What if we could resurrect Plato to grill Hawking on black holes and the soul? Using cutting-edge generative AI, we made it happen. From humble uploads to a growing audience of thinkers worldwide, Comiflix has become a beacon for intellectual curiosity in the digital age.

Jan 2024

Genesis

First AI debate: Socrates vs. Darwin on evolution and ethics.

Jun 2024

Launch

YouTube channel goes live with 10 inaugural episodes.

Sep 2025

Milestone

1M subscribers and website hub launch.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To pioneer a new era of education where AI facilitates eternal dialogues, bridging divides and igniting minds across the globe.

Mission

To produce high-quality, accessible content that provokes thought, fosters empathy, and explores the human condition through the lens of technology and tradition.

Goals

Expand to 100 episodes, launch interactive AI chat, and host live global debates by 2026.
